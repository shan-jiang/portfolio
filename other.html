Film Production

The Chian

The Chian was a music video created during Visual Story course in Entertainment Technology Center of Carnegie Mellon University. This video won The 38th Telly Awards - 2017 Bronze Telly Winner. I participated in this project as an actress and an editor.


Strange Life

Jess and Winston are two films made by a 5-members team during my undergraduate Visual Language course. I took responsibility as the director and main cameraman inside the team.
In Jess, the audience could see an inner world of a girl who feels desperate for love. In this film, what we tried to do was to make the abstract feelings and emotions be visually shown to the audience. Winston is a different story, in which there is a terrifying outside world where individuals are observed all the time. This film is more realistic. However, two films together were made to discuss one same problem: Why people feel unsafe and isolated in city life, and this is the title "Strange Life" coming from.
