VR RPG

Engine: Unity       Platform: Hololens       Duration: 3 months       Language: C#

I designed the storyline and narration in this game prototype. Player is trapped into magic castle and he need to get out of here to save his lover. In rpg game there are lots of context to read, while I find that in vr reading is not easy. I implement an interaction that user can use Sword to touch letters. The letters will disappear and show up in front of user eyes to make it easier to read. After reading, user can just touch the letter to let it go. I also try a new way of release magic power in VR: Draw patterns to spell magic on weapon (it use deep learning and pattern recognition technology)
