Design Brief

Taste Of Canton is a themed Graphic Design for Mobile phones interface. Canton, which is also called Guangzhou, is a city in south China and also my hometown. I combined my understanding of Cantonese culture and memory with Graphics, creating unique icons and interfaces.

Reference Collection

Before drawing sketches for design, I collected a lot of historical photos and pictures in real life. I analyzed different kinds of objects and picked out some of them with obvious feature relating to Canton. And then I observed the details of objects and created rough sketches.
