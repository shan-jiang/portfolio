This is a storytelling project with Microsoft HoloLens. The guest anticipates in the experience as a assistant of FBI to question suspects, finding out the killer among all characters. In this detective experience, the guest puts on Microsoft HoloLens to get the information and clues, following which the guest can figure out the truth of the story.
My main role in this project was designing the UX flow for guests to walk through the narrative successfully. And I also designed motion graphics for UI.

Experience Design

Narrative Analysis

After we had the full story, we drew out the relationship map with personal information of the story for finding the key plots in the narrative. As the guest's reaction varies a lot, it was difficult to predict what would the guest ask. By simplifying the story to key plots, we could give the characters main lines to guide the guest, letting them do improvisational acting freely for the rest of the performance. Therefore, in the whole experience, we actually followed a main storyline strictly to control the time and process of the storytelling.

AR Interface Design

In order to fit the narrative environment, we chose sci-fi style as our Interfaces style. We put the buttons in front of or near every character to let the guest check the information easily. To make a full use of the interaction of HoloLens, the guest can zoom out pictures by looking at them besides click on the objects and buttons. To build up a fully immersive environment, we combined clues and evidence with the real world, make maximal use of AR technology.
